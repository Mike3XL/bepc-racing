PaddleRace — Visualization Concepts

Real data from PNW Regional, Kayak-1 category, 2023–2026. These are concept demos — not yet in the live site.

Concept 1

Performance Heatmap — Who's Hot?

Each cell = one racer × one race. Green = beat projection, red = missed. Blank = didn't race. Reveals streaks, slumps, and who shows up consistently.

Hover a cell for details
Concept 2

Index Trajectories — The Journey

How each racer's performance index evolved over time. Lower = faster relative to field. Shows improvement arcs, plateaus, and comebacks.

Concept 3

Personal Performance Calendar

Every race as a dot. Green = beat projection, red = missed, grey = didn't race that day. Shows seasonal patterns and consistency at a glance.

Concept 4

Head-to-Head Rivalry

In races where both paddlers competed, who had the better indexed result? Bar length = win count. Click a pair to see their history.
